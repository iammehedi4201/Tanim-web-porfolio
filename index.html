Project Title :Skin Disease Detection Using Deep Learning

Objectives:

Its objective is to identify skin lesions based on texture analysis of input skin images based on deep learning for skin disease detection and diagnosis.

Motivations:

Skin diseases are more common than other diseases. But the cost of such a diagnosis is still limited and very expensive. So, the image processing techniques with the help of deep learning will be useful for early-stage skin disease detection.

Methodology:

We have proposed an image processing-based method for skin disease detection. This procedure takes a digital image of the disease affecting the area of ​​the skin, then identifies the type of disease.

Expected output:

So, based on the provided photo, we may anticipate that it will be able to determine the sort of skin illness.
